0.5.1
12 May 2010		 8
bugs		 Thanks to bugs filed by Jens Seidel and Chris Conway, Jeff Johnston fixed bugs in our Autotools component. Jeff also fixed bugs in our ChangeLog and libhover components and included the Autotools plugin documentation which was mistakenly missed in 0.5.0. Roland Grunberg fixed some OProfile bugs and committed a patch submitted by Lawrie Nichols in bug #296228.
